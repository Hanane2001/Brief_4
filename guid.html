HOW TO PLAY

Build Your Deck – Each player uses a 9-card deck with Pokémon, Energy, and Trainer cards.

Battle Pokémon – Use your Active Pokémon to attack your opponent’s and try to Knock Out theirs.

Take Prize Cards – Every time you Knock Out an opponent’s Pokémon, take one Prize card.

Win the Game – The first player to take all 6 Prize cards or leave the opponent with no Pokémon wins!
