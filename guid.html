HOW TO PLAY

Build Your Deck – Each player uses a 9-card deck with Pokémon, Energy, and Trainer cards.

Battle Pokémon – Use your Active Pokémon to attack your opponent’s and try to Knock Out theirs.

Take Prize Cards – Every time you Knock Out an opponent’s Pokémon, take one Prize card.

Win the Game – The first player to take all 6 Prize cards or leave the opponent with no Pokémon wins!

FAQ

Got questions? We’ve got answers to help you catch them all!

What is the Pokémon Card Game? The Pokémon Card Game is a collectible card game where players build decks of Pokémon, Trainer, and Energy cards to battle each other and collect Prize cards.
How do I start my collection? Start by buying a starter deck or booster packs. Collect cards, organize them, and gradually build a stronger deck.
Are there rules for beginners? Yes, the Guide page explains all the rules step by step. Start with basic Pokémon and simple strategies, then learn advanced tactics as you play.
